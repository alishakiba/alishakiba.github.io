Ali Shakiba Assistant Professor of Computer Science
Vali-e-Asr University of Rafsanjan
Rafsanjan, Iran

I am an assistant professor of computer science at Vali-e-Asr university of Rafsanjan, Iran. I have a broad research experience including parameterized complexity, machine learning, chaotic cryptography and computer vision. Before joining the VRU as a faculty, I have spent 5 years and a half at Yazd university to obtain my PhD and MSc degrees and 4 years at Shahid Bahonar university of Kerman to do my BSc, all in computer science.
