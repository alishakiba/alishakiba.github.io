Computational Data Mining - Fall 2019

  • Reference: Marc Peter Deisenroth, A Aldo Faisal, and Cheng Soon Ong. 2020. Mathematics for Machine Learning. Cambridge University Press. - The authors generously provided a free version of their book at https://mml-book.github.io.
  • We use the scientific programming stack of Python. I strongly suggest download and install Anaconda Python 3.7. Throughout the course, we use thefollowing libraries: numpy, scipy, matplotlib, scikit-learn, pandas, tensorflow, keras, fast.ai, and many others.
